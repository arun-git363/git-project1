Snapdeal is an Indian e-commerce platform founded in 2010 by Kunal Bahl and Rohit Bansal.

It offers a wide variety of products including clothing, electronics, home goods, and accessories at affordable prices.

Snapdeal focuses on providing value-for-money products, especially catering to customers in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.

The platform operates mainly as a marketplace, connecting sellers with buyers across India.

Despite strong competition, Snapdeal continues to serve millions of customers with its focus on budget-friendly shopping.
